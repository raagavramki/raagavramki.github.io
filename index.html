About Me

I'm a B.Tech Honours student in Electronics and Communication at IIIT Hyderabad, passionate about the intersection of technology and human experience. My journey spans from low-level hardware design to cutting-edge machine learning applications.

Currently, I'm conducting interdisciplinary research on how technology shapes perceptions of well-being in fitness and bodybuilding, bridging technological design with socio-cultural insights within an Indian context.

Beyond academics, I lead the Debate Club at IIIT-H, mentoring 30+ members and organizing tournaments. I've also delivered a TEDx talk on "Clarity in a Changing World," exploring tech-society relations.
